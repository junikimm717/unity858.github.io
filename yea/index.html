Youth EUCLID | About

Youth EUCLID Association (better known locally as "YEA") is an in-person math org located in East Bay, California, founded by Justin Lee (eisirrational) and Sean Li (Th3Numb3rThr33) and their parents.
(Yes, it even owns two buildings lmao)

We've hosted many of today's renowned math students, including several MOPpers. Also, we've sent teams and winning awards to various college math contests , including a sweep of CHMMC 2021.
